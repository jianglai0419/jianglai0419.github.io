AURA

灵韵 一种超越于自身的某种东西，带着神秘的经验，穿越时空，时而隐蔽时而闪现。

当我们企图询问一张塔罗牌以得到指引时，灵光便从牌面上浮现，它来自于画面散发的氛围，来自于对于传统的认知，也来自于我们的过往经验。灵光闪现之时，答案已然浮现，决断也已然产生，带领我们向前走的，是命运之手轻轻的一推。 灵光隐去，正如它揭示了又掩盖去，但我们已然迈出了原地，在下一个时空，在你需要的时候，我们与灵光，再次相遇。

Aura is a spirit that transcends, with mysterious experience, travels through time and space, and flashes from time to time in secret. When we tried to inquire about a tarot card to get guidance, the aura emerged from the card. It came from the atmosphere of the artwork, from traditional cognition, and our experience. When the aura flashed, the answer had already emerged, and we had already made a decision. What led us forward? It's a gentle push by the hand of destiny. The aura disappears, just as it reveals, but we have already stepped out. The next time and space, when we need aura, we will meet again.
